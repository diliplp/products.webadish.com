FAQ

Key buyer questions, answered clearly

Is this really a one-time payment?

Yes. Unlike Zapier and other SaaS-based connectors, WebAdish AutoSheets Pro is sold with one-time pricing and lifetime updates.

Do I need a Google account?

Yes. Data goes from your WordPress site to your own Google account. WebAdish does not see or store your form submissions.

How many submissions can I sync?

Unlimited. Both free and Pro editions can handle unlimited submissions, subject to Google Sheets limits.

Can I use this on client sites?

Yes. The 5-site and unlimited licenses are intended for freelancers and agencies working across multiple installs.

What if I need help?

Pro users get priority support with a 24-hour response target via email. Free users can use the WordPress.org forums.

Can I upgrade from free to Pro?

Yes. Once you buy Pro, activate the license inside the plugin settings and continue from your existing setup.
