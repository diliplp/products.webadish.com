Support

Support structure for AutoSheets free users, Pro buyers, and agency customers.

Keep support expectations clear from day one so plugin growth does not turn into inbox chaos.

Free Plugin Support

Route free users to WordPress.org support forums and public docs for Contact Form 7 setup and basic plugin questions.

Pro Support

Pro users get priority support within 24 hours via email for setup, upgrades, sync logic, and integration troubleshooting.

Commercial / Agency Support

Reserved for 5-site and unlimited buyers who need help across multiple client installs or larger operational workflows.

Response Targets

Free support target: 48 hours. Pro support target: 24 hours. Track first response time, resolution time, and ticket volume weekly.

Common Issues

Typical issues include Apps Script deployment mistakes, webhook URL errors, plugin detection problems, and Google authorization failures.

What To Include

WordPress version, PHP version, form plugin name, Activity Log error, screenshots, and the steps already attempted.
