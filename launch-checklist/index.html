AutoSheets Launch Checklist

This page captures the deployment and growth checklist from the supplied launch plan so the rollout stays organized after WordPress.org approval.

Immediate Actions

  • Submit the free Contact Form 7 plugin to WordPress.org and monitor approval turnaround.
  • Keep the Pro sales page, docs, and legal pages live and tested on plugins.webadish.com.
  • Verify product emails exist: support, sales, refunds, privacy, legal, abuse, and GDPR.
  • Install analytics tracking for page views and pricing-button clicks.

Week 1 After Approval

  • Create WordPress.org banner, icon, and screenshots.
  • Set up Razorpay and Stripe payment flows.
  • Replace email purchase links with actual checkout or payment links.
  • Implement the first license generation and activation workflow.

Build Roadmap

  • Add remaining Pro integrations: Gravity Forms, Formidable, Ninja Forms, Elementor Forms, and Fluent Forms.
  • Ship license activation, multiple spreadsheet routing, field mapping, and conditional sync rules.
  • Validate multi-site licensing and update delivery.
  • Keep a troubleshooting knowledge base updated as launch issues appear.

Marketing

  • Publish supporting blog content for WPForms, Gravity Forms, and Contact Form 7 Google Sheets use cases.
  • Create a YouTube setup tutorial with Apps Script walkthrough and Pro upsell.
  • Build Google Ads and social campaigns around high-intent integration queries.
  • Answer WordPress.org support forums and review feedback consistently.

Revenue Milestones

  • Month 1 target: ₹50,000
  • Month 3 target: ₹1,50,000
  • Month 6 target: ₹3,00,000
  • Year 1 target: ₹20,00,000+

Support Infrastructure

  • Free users: WordPress.org forums with 48-hour response target.
  • Pro users: email support with 24-hour response target.
  • Track first response time, resolution time, ticket volume, and customer satisfaction.
  • Keep a repeatable triage process for bugs, how-to issues, and license problems.
