Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026
Last Updated: March 25, 2026

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

WebAdish AutoSheets Pro includes a full refund window of 30 days from purchase if the plugin does not work as described or you are unsatisfied for any reason.

2. How To Request a Refund

Email refunds@webadish.com with your full name, purchase email, license key, and an optional reason for the refund request.

Response target: 2 business days. Refund processing target: within 7 business days after approval.

3. Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method, whether that is Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal, or the original card/account used during purchase.

4. What Happens After Refund

If a refund is approved, the license key is deactivated. You may continue using the free version where applicable, but Pro-only features and updates will no longer remain licensed.

5. Exceptions

  • No refunds after 30 days.
  • No refunds where license keys were shared or resold.
  • No refunds for Terms of Service violations.
  • Fraudulent chargebacks are treated as abuse and may lead to permanent purchase restrictions.

6. Upgrades and Multiple Licenses

Multiple licenses are treated individually. Upgrade purchases reset the refund window for the upgrade itself, not the original purchase amount.

7. Technical Issues

If you hit a technical issue, contact support@webadish.com first. The support team will try to resolve the issue before a refund is necessary.

8. Contact

Refund questions can be sent to refunds@webadish.com. Pre-purchase questions can be sent to sales@webadish.com.
